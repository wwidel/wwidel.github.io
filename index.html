Wojciech (Wojtek) Wideł

I am a postdoctoral researcher, working on security modeling at KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Stockholm, Sweden), within the Division of Network and System Engineering.

Previously, I was a doctoral student in computer science within the EMSEC team at IRISA (Rennes, France). I was working on formal foundations of a graphical model for security called attack–defense trees, under the supervision of Barbara Kordy. I should obtain a Ph.D. in computer science in December 2019.

In May 2017 I obtained a Ph.D. in mathematics, from AGH UST in Kraków, Poland, where I worked with A. Paweł Wojda.

e-mail: widel [at] kth [dot] se








Research interests: threat modeling, attack and attack-defense trees, sufficient conditions for Hamiltonicity of simple graphs
